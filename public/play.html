Play Atari Go

Rules

This game is known as Atari, or Capture Go. Black starts the game. Each player puts a stone on a square in their turn. Stones of the same color are connected in rows and columns. A stone is live as long as it has a connection with an empty square, or when it is the member of the group where at least one other member connects with an empty square. Stone(s) without connection to an empty square are dead, and are captured. The first player who makes a capture, wins.

To start playing, click on a player's name. When the name is grey, they are waiting for a challenge. If the name is blue, they are currently playing.
